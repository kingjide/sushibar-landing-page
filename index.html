Why SusiBar

The story of why we were founded/h3>

  • 🥢

    Birthed from our love of sushi and tech

    We love sushi and technology and notticed many of our favorite sushibars didnt have a good website to scope the menu and call in ahead of time <

  • 🍣

    SushiBar is Born

    We purchased .sushibar/ on the Handshake blockchain through Namebase.io and began to make static websites for our local sushi bars ins Santa Barbara

  • The Decentralized Internet

    Begin development of static framework websites that can be easily deployed for a low cost

  • Next Phase

    Reach out

    Currently in the process of finding new partners in the sushi industry who want to test our sites and services for no cost
